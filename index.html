Devices that I use

by Nitin Chaudhary


Asus ROG Strix G15

Ask a Gamer what his favourite Gaming sub-brand is , and 9 out of 10 would say the name ROG. My choice and dream since 2019(when I first heard the name ROG) finally came true last October. The Ryzen 4800H Octa-core processor coupled with nVIDIA RTX 3050 is surely a BEAST. Also it has an 8 GB RAM , a 512 GB SSD and a 144 Hz refresh rate display

Currently I use it for attending Online classes , Coding , Youtube and Gaming.

Keep in mind that there is no Webcam. Also since Win 11 update some glitches are present


4.5⭐/5.

Poco X3 Pro

Without Phone life is difficult. But it is more difficult when your phone has a disease of Hanging. My old phone(Galaxy M20) was counting its last days due to this sickness. So I went to buy a new one and the phone which really caught my eye(and in budget obviously) was this POCO X3 Pro. The SnapDragon 860 processor and 6GB RAM(8 GB was out of stock unfortunately) is what you require for a Good BGMI session. Has a decent camera and battery .

MIUI Needs some serious improvements. It also has some glitches


Rating 4⭐/5

Realme Buds Wireless 2

See the colour Yellow and the first Brand which comes in anayone's mind is REALME.The Buds Wireless 2 opts for a rubberised, silicone chassis that is soft to the touch. The earphones ship with a pair of 13.6mm drivers, offer support for Sony’s LDAC codec , and are high-res certified . No need to be worried about sweat or water splashes when using the Buds as the earphones are IPX5 certified as well. The ANC(Active noice cancellation) also works brilliantly.

Battery is somewhat less as compared to other neckbands in similar price range.


Rating 4⭐/5
